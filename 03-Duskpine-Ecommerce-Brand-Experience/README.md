# Duskpine — Quiet, Naturally

> **UX/UI Case Study | Brand Identity | E-commerce Experience | Social Media Campaign**

## Overview

Duskpine is a conceptual direct-to-consumer (DTC) wellness brand offering handcrafted natural beeswax noise-cancelling earplugs. This project explores the complete UX/UI design process, from building a premium brand identity to designing a responsive e-commerce landing page, a streamlined checkout experience, and cohesive social media marketing assets.

The primary objective was to create a user-centred digital experience that educates visitors, builds trust, and encourages purchase through intuitive design and compelling visual storytelling.

---

## Project Objectives

* Design a premium and memorable brand identity.
* Create a responsive, conversion-focused landing page.
* Design a simple and frictionless checkout experience.
* Maintain visual consistency across digital touchpoints.
* Develop social media assets aligned with the brand identity.
