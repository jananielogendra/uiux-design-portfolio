# Base360 — AI Conversations, Reimagined

A UX/UI case study for designing a modern AI SaaS landing page focused on communicating product value, building user trust, and driving conversions.

---

## Project Overview

Base360 is an AI-powered communication platform that unifies customer conversations across multiple channels into one intelligent system.

This case study demonstrates the complete UX/UI design process—from research and planning to high-fidelity UI design and responsive adaptation.

---

## Objectives

- Simplify a complex AI product through clear storytelling
- Design a high-converting SaaS landing page
- Build user trust using product previews and social proof
- Create a scalable design system
- Deliver a responsive experience across devices
